Description

The form appliction below allows you to get your akan name .With the use of your birthday informations and your gender one will be able to acces their akan names.Akan names are derived from Ghananian culture.Frequently in Ghana people are given their firstname as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day of the week in which they were born in.Most people do know when their birthdays are, but do not know the week of the day in which they were born in.

Day of the week (d) = ( ( (CC/4) -2*CC-1) + ((5*YY/4) ) + ((26*(MM+1)/10)) + DD ) mod 7

where;

CC - is the century digits. For example 1989 has CC = 19

YY - is the Year digits (1989 has YY = 89)

MM - is the Month

DD - is the Day of the month

mod - is the modulus function ( % )
